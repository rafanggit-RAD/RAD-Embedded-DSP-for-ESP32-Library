===================================================================
LIST KONFIGURASI PIN HARDWARE - RAD_DSP_AMP
===================================================================
Project: RAD_DSP_AMP
Platform: ESP32
Description: DSP Amplifier with PCM1754 DAC, PCM1808 ADC, OLED, 
             Rotary Encoder, and Indicator LEDs.
===================================================================

1. ANTARMUKA AUDIO I2S (PCM1754 DAC & PCM1808 ADC)
-------------------------------------------------------------------
Fungsi Pin       | ESP32 Pin GPIO | Keterangan
-------------------------------------------------------------------
I2S_MCLK_PIN     | GPIO 0         | Master Clock
I2S_WS_PIN       | GPIO 4         | Word Select (LRCK / Left-Right Clock)
I2S_BCK_PIN      | GPIO 16        | Bit Clock (SCK)
I2S_DOUT_PIN     | GPIO 17        | Data Output (ESP32 -> PCM1754 DAC)
I2S_DIN_PIN      | GPIO 34        | Data Input (PCM1808 ADC -> ESP32)

2. ANTARMUKA LAYAR OLED (I2C)
-------------------------------------------------------------------
Fungsi Pin       | ESP32 Pin GPIO | Keterangan
-------------------------------------------------------------------
OLED_SDA         | GPIO 14        | I2C Serial Data
OLED_SCL         | GPIO 12        | I2C Serial Clock

3. ROTARY ENCODER
-------------------------------------------------------------------
Fungsi Pin       | ESP32 Pin GPIO | Keterangan
-------------------------------------------------------------------
ENC_CLK          | GPIO 25        | Encoder Clock (A Channel) - Interrupt
ENC_DT           | GPIO 26        | Encoder Data (B Channel) - Interrupt
ENC_SW           | GPIO 27        | Encoder Switch / Button (Debounced)

4. LED INDIKATOR (LEDC PWM)
-------------------------------------------------------------------
Fungsi Pin       | ESP32 Pin GPIO | Keterangan
-------------------------------------------------------------------
LED_AUDIO        | GPIO 32        | Audio Peak Indicator (PWM 5kHz, 8-bit)
LED_BT           | GPIO 33        | Bluetooth Status Indicator (PWM 5kHz, 8-bit)

===================================================================
Catatan Penggunaan & Fitur Hardware:
- Protokol I2S dijalankan pada Core 0 dengan sample rate 48000 Hz, 32-bit resolution.
- Rotary Encoder menggunakan Register-level Interrupt Handler untuk presisi tinggi dan
  menghindari cache miss panic pada ESP32.
- Bluetooth Status LED menggunakan Breathing effect pada pin 33 saat pairing/idle dan 
  solid ON saat terhubung.
